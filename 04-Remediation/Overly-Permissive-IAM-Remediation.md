# Remediation – Overly Permissive IAM Policies

## Objective

Reduce unnecessary permissions and ensure that users, groups, and roles have only the access required to perform their job functions.

---

## Immediate Actions

- Identify accounts with excessive permissions.
- Remove unnecessary administrative privileges.
- Review recently modified IAM policies.
- Disable unused accounts.

---

## Access Management

- Apply the Principle of Least Privilege.
- Implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
- Separate administrative and standard user accounts.
- Use temporary privilege elevation where appropriate.

---

## Monitoring

- Enable audit logging for IAM changes.
- Monitor privileged account activity.
- Review access logs regularly.
- Generate alerts for unexpected privilege changes.

---

## Long-Term Recommendations

- Conduct periodic IAM reviews.
- Remove inactive accounts.
- Enforce Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).
- Perform regular privilege audits.
- Review IAM policies as part of change management.
