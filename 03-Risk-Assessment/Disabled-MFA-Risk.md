# Risk Assessment – Disabled Multi-Factor Authentication

## Risk Rating

**High**

---

## Likelihood

High

Credential theft remains one of the most common attack methods. Accounts protected only by passwords are significantly more vulnerable.

---

## Impact

High

Compromised accounts may allow attackers to access sensitive cloud resources and potentially escalate privileges.

---

## Overall Risk

High

Implementing MFA substantially reduces the likelihood of successful credential-based attacks.

---

## Recommended Priority

High

MFA should be enforced across all cloud accounts, with administrator accounts receiving the highest priority.
