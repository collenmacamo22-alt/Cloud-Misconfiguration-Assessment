# Risk Assessment – Misconfigured Security Groups

## Risk Rating

**High**

---

## Likelihood

High

Misconfigured firewall rules are frequently discovered during cloud security assessments and are commonly exploited by attackers performing Internet-wide scanning.

---

## Impact

High

Successful exploitation may result in unauthorized system access, malware deployment, or compromise of cloud workloads.

---

## Overall Risk

High

Restricting unnecessary network exposure significantly reduces the attack surface.

---

## Recommended Priority

High

Internet-facing management ports should be reviewed and restricted immediately.
