# Risk Assessment – Publicly Accessible Storage

## Risk Rating

**Critical**

---

## Likelihood

High

Public storage misconfigurations are among the most common cloud security issues and frequently result from configuration mistakes rather than sophisticated attacks.

Attackers routinely scan the Internet looking for exposed cloud storage.

---

## Impact

Critical

Successful exploitation may expose confidential company data without requiring attackers to compromise user accounts or exploit software vulnerabilities.

---

## Overall Risk

Critical

The combination of a high likelihood of discovery and severe business impact makes publicly accessible cloud storage one of the highest-risk cloud security misconfigurations.

---

## Recommended Priority

Immediate remediation.

Any publicly exposed storage containing sensitive information should be secured as soon as possible.
